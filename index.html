Our Mission

Nayee Subah Foundation will seek to enhance and advance the lives of under privileged children and families in need .

Our Vision

Our vision is to give every child in Pakistan chance for transforming his or her life through education. We strive to improve the quality of education for talented, but underprivileged children of Pakistan.

Courses We Offer

Daraz MarketPlace

Daraz is South Asia’s leading virtual marketplace that has more than 20 million products in 100+ categories on its website and App.

Graphic design

Graphic design is a craft where professionals create visual content to communicate messages.

WordPress

WordPress is an excellent website building platform for a variety of websites. Designed with usability and flexibility in mind, WordPress is a great solution for both large and small websites.

Freelancing

Freelancing is actually the future where everyone can sell their skill and make money online. Freelancing is a type of self-employment.

Digital Marketing

Through this course, you’ll learn about Content Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Email Marketing, Mobile Marketing, and Digital Campaigns.

Basic Computer

This course helps you in learning office productivity tools that are part and parcel of modern work environment. It will provide hands on training in Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel & Microsoft PowerPoint. These applications fit in all types of the modern working environment.

Web Designing

In this course, we explain the basic tools that every web page coder needs to know. We will start from the ground up by learning how to implement modern web pages with HTML, CSS & Bootstrap.

MCTP

MCTP MCTP is mobile computer training program. Under this program, computer education is provided Free of Cost in schools where such facility is not available. Since September 2018, 3563 students of different Schools have completed the training.
